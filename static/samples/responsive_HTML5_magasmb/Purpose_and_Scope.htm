Purpose and Scope

This manual contains procedures for assembling the 6-in. magnet for the magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) logging tool. Carefully follow the steps in these procedures to work safely and assemble all magnets consistently.

The procedures in this manual are specific to the 6-in. magnet. If you are assembling the 8-in. magnet, please use the MRI Logging Magnet Assembly Manual (8-in), document no. XXX-XX-XX-XXXX.
