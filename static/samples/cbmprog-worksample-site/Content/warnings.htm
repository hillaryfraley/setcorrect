Warnings

Printed circuit boards (PCBs) are electrostatic-sensitive devices (ESDs). To avoid damaging the PCB, wear an approved wrist strap that is connected to a properly grounded workbench.

To help prevent electric shock, inspect cords and equipment for damage before plugging in.
